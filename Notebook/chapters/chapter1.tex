\section{Mathematical Equations}

Some mathematical equations:

\begin{equation}
    E = mc^2
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
    \frac{d}{dx} \left( e^{x} \right) = e^{x}
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
    \int_{a}^{b} x^2 \, dx = \left[ \frac{x^3}{3} \right]_{a}^{b}
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
    \vec{F} = m \vec{a}
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
    \sigma = \frac{F}{A}
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
    \sum_{n=1}^{N} n = \frac{N(N+1)}{2}
\end{equation}
